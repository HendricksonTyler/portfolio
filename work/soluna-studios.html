Soluna Studios

Logo Design & Branding - Work in Progress

Soluna Studios is a development studio that my brother and I are forming as a side project of sorts. As such, I took it upon myself to come up with the name and branding for the company. This involved designing the logo, picking the color scheme, and so on.

The Logo

Soluna is a combination of two latin words: "sol" meaning sun and "luna" meaning moon. The logo was designed to reflect this. The "eclipse" logo is made up of a sun with a corona to one side and encompassing a smaller crescent moon within it. It is symmetrical about the crescent at a 45-degree angle.

Color Scheme

My two favorite colors are blue and orange, so naturally, I wanted to incorporate these colors in some way. Luckily, blue and orange are perfect colors to represent the moon and sun. As complementary colors, blue and orange do well to contrast one another—just as sun and the moon contrast each other.

White Variant

Any good logo needs to be versatile. The logo for Soluna Studios also has a monochrome variant allowing it to look good on a colored background or image.

Text

Just as important as the logo, a good, clean-looking typeface is essential for the look of a business entity. In addition to being free to use, we selected Roboto because of its clean lines and thin text. Because "sol" represents the sun and "luna" represents the moon, we always color the word Soluna in orange and blue respectively.
