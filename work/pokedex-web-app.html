Pokédex Web App

Web Development - Work in Progress

This is a something that I am currently working on as a side project under Soluna Studios. This web app aims to assist both the competitive and casual Pokémon communities by providing a quick and efficient way to access the information they need.

The Problem

Many of the top Pokédex sites popular today leave much to be desired. Either they are loaded with tons of information that you might not even need or they are so skimpy and light that you don't find the details you are looking for. Making matters worse, many aren't optimized for mobile at all.

Examples of these sites include the popular Serebii.net and Bulbapedia sites.

The Solution

Highly Scalable

With so much of today's web traffic coming from mobile devices, making the experience good on mobile is of prime importance. Of course, we don't neglect tablet and desktop users; the app dynamically changes to any screen size to optimize usability and screen real estate.

Lightning Quick

With all of the information involved in Pokémon, it's essential that the process be quick and easy to find what you're looking for. By building the app in the just-released AngularJS 2, pages will only load what they need to and never disrupt the user. By leveraging the advanced technology in today's browsers, pages can also be built on the fly.

Easy to Use

No longer will you have to put up with pinching and zooming to read the tiny text like the other sites. By cutting out all the fluff, the user will only be presented with the information that truly matters. This allows us to make things big, readable, and (most importantly) usable on any device. In addition, users will also be able to save the web app to their home screen where it will function like a native app.

Try It Out

Although the app is far from being completed, I've included a couple of example pages for you to flip through. Even though it is very simple thus far, I hope you can get a sense for the seamless experience that the Pokédex aims to achieve.
